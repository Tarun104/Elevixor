Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What does ATS mean and why is it important?
ATS stands for Applicant Tracking System. Most companies use ATS to filter resumes before they reach human recruiters. Our optimization ensures your resume passes the ATS scan and reaches hiring managers.
How long does resume optimization take?
You get instant ATS analysis and recommendations right after uploading your resume. Detailed optimization reports are typically ready within 24 hours.
Can I customize the portfolio templates?
Yes! All our portfolio templates are fully customizable. You can change colors, add your projects, modify text, and deploy them on your domain.
What if I'm not satisfied with the service?
We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with any service, contact our support team and we'll process a full refund.
Do you provide career counseling?
Yes! Our Career Starter Pack includes interview preparation, domain guidance, and personalized recommendations to help you grow professionally.
