FAQ

Questions? We've Got Answers

Everything you need to know before starting your project with us.

How long does a typical project take?
+
Project timelines vary by scope. A standard website takes 4–6 weeks, a web application 8–16 weeks, and a full mobile app 12–20 weeks. We always provide a detailed timeline in your project proposal after our discovery call.
Do you work with clients outside your local area?
+
Absolutely. We work with clients across 40+ countries. Our remote collaboration process is refined and efficient — weekly video calls, real-time project management tools, and asynchronous communication ensure seamless collaboration regardless of time zones.
What technologies do you specialize in?
+
We're technology-agnostic and choose the best stack for each project. Our core expertise spans React, Next.js, Node.js, Python, React Native, Flutter, AWS, Google Cloud, and a wide range of AI/ML frameworks including TensorFlow and OpenAI integrations.
Do you offer ongoing support after launch?
+
Yes, all plans include post-launch support. Our Starter plan includes 3 months of support, Professional includes 12 months, and Enterprise includes unlimited ongoing support with guaranteed response times. We also offer standalone maintenance retainers.
Can I see examples of work in my industry?
+
Definitely. We have detailed case studies for most of the industries we serve. During our discovery call, we'll walk you through relevant examples and discuss how we've solved similar challenges for businesses like yours.
What's your payment structure?
+
We typically work with a 40% upfront deposit, 30% at the midpoint milestone, and 30% upon project completion. For ongoing retainer work, invoicing is monthly. We accept wire transfers, credit cards, and crypto payments.
